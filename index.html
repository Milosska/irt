About event

International Round Table «Sustainable consumption in restaurant industry» is an annual event dedicated to global problems and challenges faced by modern restaurant business. It brings together experts from around the world to discuss issues related to overconsumption and excessive production, to share international experience and to stimulate sustainable production and service practices.

Organizers

The event is organized by the Department of Technology of Restaurant and Ayurvedic Products of National University of Food Technologies, Kyiv, Ukraine, jointly with Lankaran State University of the Ministry of Education of the Republic of Azerbaijan and Sri Konda Laxman Telangana State Horticultural University, Telangana, India.

Who should join us?

    Discuss new technologies, socialize with fellow researchers all over the world, and explore new opportunities for your career.

  • Business

    Learn about the latest developments and technologies that can help in enhancing the quality of the food products.
